The smart way to fight food insecurity .

Join us in the fight against hunger. Our platform predicts and manages food donations, streamlines inventory management, and connects volunteers to food banks in real-time.

Predict and manage food donations .

Our AI technology predicts the types and quantities of food donations, allowing food banks to plan more effectively and reduce waste.

Real-time inventory management .

With Smart Food Bank, managing inventory is a breeze. Our platform allows food banks to track inventory in real-time, reducing the risk of shortages and waste.

Connect with volunteers .

Our platform connects food banks to volunteers in real-time, making it easier to coordinate pick-ups, deliveries, and other tasks. Volunteers can also track their impact on the community and earn rewards for their efforts.
